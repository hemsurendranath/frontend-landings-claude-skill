FAQ

Common Questions

We currently support Arbin (.csv), Neware (.csv), BioLogic (.mpt), and Maccor (.txt/.csv) formats. Our universal parser auto-detects the format on upload. We're continuously adding new formats — if yours isn't listed, contact us and we'll build a custom parser as part of your onboarding.

Our supervised ML model is trained on 500+ manually labelled battery cycles across NMC, LFP, and NCA chemistries. It uses 37 engineered features based on battery physics — voltage derivatives, coulomb counting, power profiles — to identify charge/discharge cycles with over 95% accuracy, including edge cases like partial cycles and interrupted tests.

Think of it as a "nutrition label" for batteries. Under EU Regulation 2023/1542, every battery sold in the EU must have a machine-readable passport containing performance metrics (capacity, efficiency, resistance), identification data, and sustainability information. It's defined by the DIN SPEC 99100 standard and is mandatory from February 2027.
